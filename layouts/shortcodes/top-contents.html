Physician Roster Challenge

Provider Rosters' changes are difficult to capture and harder to share in a Physician practice. Roster updates are done on EHR/EMR, RCM and other systems, but are not integrated with Insurance Plans or with the Physician practice websites. This lack of automation adds cost, in addition to reducing productivity and sometimes even leading to revenue loss and an unsatisfactory patient experience. Insurance providers are on the hook for CMS penalties and so they spend money on manual cross checks to ensure that the rosters are up to date. Despite this, sometimes they have unsatisfactory member experiences leading to lost customers.

RosterSource Platform as a Service (PaaS) open source solution

RosterSource Platform solves this problem by bringing real-time Provider roster data from various internal Physician Practice systems securely to authorized and Practice approved RosterSource users on the RosterSource network. These users can be other peer Physician Practices or plans that individual providers have contracted with, for healthcare services. The history of all the roster changes including RosterSource address duplication, merger and conflict resolution of the Physicians roster is maintained.
